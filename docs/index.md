# Retro Monitor Repair Wiki

Welcome to the definitive resource for repairing vintage CRT monitors. This wiki provides comprehensive, step-by-step repair guides for classic monitors from the golden age of computing.

## Featured Monitor: Commodore Amiga 1080

The Commodore Amiga 1080 is a 12-inch RGB monitor that was bundled with the Amiga 1000 computer. Known for its crisp display and vibrant colors, it remains a favorite among retro computing enthusiasts.

## ⚠️ Critical Safety Warning

**CRT monitors contain lethal voltages that can cause serious injury or death!**

Before attempting any repairs:

1. **Always discharge the picture tube anode** to the CRT conductive coating
2. **Use proper high-voltage probes** and insulated tools
3. **Work in a well-ventilated area**
4. **Never work alone** on high-voltage equipment
5. **If unsure, consult a qualified technician**

The information provided here is for educational purposes only. Repairing CRT monitors should only be attempted by individuals with proper training and experience in electronics repair.
